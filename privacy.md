# RemindYou Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-13_

## What we collect

When you create a RemindYou account, we collect:

- **Email address and password** (or Google account identifier if you use Google Sign-In) — managed by Firebase Authentication.
- **Display name and profile photo** — optional, set by you.
- **Purchase records** — when you subscribe to RemindYou Pro, the purchase metadata (product ID, transaction ID, date, amount, currency) is processed by Adapty for subscription management.

Guests (users who use the app without signing up) provide no identifying data.

## What stays on your device

- **WiFi network names** you add to RemindYou
- **Reminder content** (titles, icons, network associations)
- **Checklist state** (which items are checked)
- **Notification preferences**

This data is stored only in your device's local storage and never transmitted to our servers.

## How we use location

RemindYou requests "Allow all the time" location permission solely to keep WiFi monitoring running in the background. We do not collect, log, transmit, or share your location coordinates. The permission is required by Android to keep our foreground service running so disconnect reminders can fire.

## How we use your data

- **Email**: to send account-related messages (welcome email, email verification, password reset, purchase receipts) and to identify you on sign-in.
- **Name and photo**: shown only in your profile screen.
- **Purchase records**: to determine whether you have an active RemindYou Pro subscription.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

## Third-party services

- **Firebase (Google LLC)** — authentication, cloud functions for transactional email. [Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Adapty** — subscription management and analytics. [Privacy](https://adapty.io/privacy/)
- **Resend** — transactional email delivery. [Privacy](https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- **Google Play Billing** — subscription payments. [Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Your rights

You can:
- Delete your account from the Profile screen — this removes your data from Firebase Authentication. Your purchase history with Adapty/Google Play is retained as required by law and store policies.
- Request a copy or deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.
- Cancel your subscription at any time through your device's Subscriptions settings (App Store on iOS, Play Store on Android).

## Children

RemindYou is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in-app.
