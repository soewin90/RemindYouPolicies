Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app — no need to contact us.

This action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please make sure you want to proceed before continuing. Howerver, you can register back again if you wish to create account. Also, you can restore purchase with your account unless its expired.

How to delete your account

1

Open the app and sign in

Make sure you are signed into the account you wish to delete.

2

Go to your Profile tab

Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar to open your account settings.

3

Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll down to find the Delete Account button. Tap it and confirm when prompted. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted.

What gets deleted

Account credentials — your email address, password, and sign-in methods

Profile information — your display name, profile photo, and preferences

App data — all content, history, and activity associated with your account

Device identifiers — any device IDs linked to your account

Can't access the app?

Contact us and we'll delete your account manually within 30 days.
