# 🗑️ Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app — no need to contact us.

---

> ⚠️ **This action is permanent.** Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please make sure you want to proceed before continuing.

---

## How to Delete Your Account

**Step 1 — Open the app and sign in**

Make sure you are signed into the account you wish to delete.

---

**Step 2 — Go to your Profile tab**

Tap the **Profile** tab in the bottom navigation bar to open your account settings.

---

**Step 3 — Tap "Delete Account"**

Scroll down to find the **Delete Account** button. Tap it and confirm when prompted. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted.

![Delete Account button in the Profile tab](screenshot.png)

---

## What Gets Deleted

| Data | Action |
|---|---|
| ✅ Account credentials | Email address, password, and sign-in methods deleted |
| ✅ Profile information | Display name, profile photo, and preferences deleted |
| ✅ App data | All content, history, and activity deleted |
| ✅ Device identifiers | Any device IDs linked to your account deleted |

Deletion is completed within **30 days** of your request.

---

## Can't Access the App?

If you no longer have access to the app, you can contact us directly and we will delete your account manually within 30 days.

📧 [support@yourapp.com](mailto:support@yourapp.com?subject=Account%20Deletion%20Request)
